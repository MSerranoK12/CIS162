About Me

My name is Maria Serrano and I am currently on summer vacation. During the summer, I plan to take three summer courses at Cerritos Community College: two computer courses and a self-defense class. The computer courses are for career advancement and the self-defense class is part of my re-entry to martial arts. About 10 years ago, I earned a blue belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and trained in Muay Thai but stopped because of injuries. I recently discovered Filipino Martial Arts, specifically Eskrima/Arnis, which is the perfect martial art for me because it is much easier on the joints.
